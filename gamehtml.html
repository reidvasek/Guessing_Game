Hobbit vs. Troll

VS.

Long after the ring of power was destroyed, a large group of hobbits are sitting at the green dragon enjoying a nice pint of brew. All of a sudden a small little hobbits runs into the bar and shouts "Troll! Troll! There is a troll in the shire and he is starting to eat all the little hobbits". All of the hobbits except for Sam, Frodo, Pippin and Merry start running and screaming. It us up to one of them to stop the troll before he eats all of the little halflings.

Directions

  1. Choose your character
  2. Choose what you will do to stop the troll
  3. Save the shire!
